# Bioinformatics Glossary

## CLL

Chronic Lymphocytic Leukemia. A blood cancer of mature B cells.

## BCR

B-cell receptor. A receptor on B cells that can identify antigens. In CLL, the malignant B cells are clonal, meaning they express the same BCR.

## SNP

Single nucleotide polymorphism. A DNA position where different people can have different nucleotides, for example C in one person and T in another person.

## Germline variant

A variant inherited from parents. It is present in the normal genome of the person.

## Somatic variant

A variant acquired during life. In cancer, somatic variants are present in tumor cells but not necessarily in normal cells.

## FASTQ

A sequencing file format containing read sequences and quality scores.

## BAM

A binary alignment file. It stores sequencing reads after they have been aligned to a reference genome.

## Reference genome

A standard human genome sequence used as a coordinate system. Reads are aligned against it.

## Alignment

The process of placing sequencing reads onto the reference genome.

## Variant calling

The process of identifying differences between the sample DNA and the reference genome.
